Shree Kripalu Kunj Ashram.
Learn about our Spiritual Master and the world's fifth original Jagadguru, Shri Kripalu Ji Maharaj, what we teach, our mission, the many locations where satsang is being conducted,learn what is Bhakti,Who is a true Guru and Radha Krishna the Divine Couple.
For the benefit of the devotees, by the grace of Shri Radha Krishna and Jagadguru Shri Kripalu Ji Maharaj, Didi ji has established this ashram in bathinda, where devotees of all walks of life, ethnicity and backgrounds regularly gather to praise God and Guru.
The word jagad (जगद्) means the entire world. Guru (गुरू) means the one capable of dispelling our ignorance and granting divine knowledge. Such a Spiritual Master who is unanimously accepted by all spiritual scholars of his age as the supreme spiritual teacher is called a Jagadguru.
