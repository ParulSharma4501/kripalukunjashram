Shree Kripalu Kunj Ashram.
For the benefit of the devotees, by the grace of
Shri Radha Krishna and Jagadguru Shri Kripalu
Ji Maharaj, Didi ji has established this ashram
in bathinda, where devotees of all walks of life,
ethnicity and backgrounds regularly gather to
praise God and Guru.
